## V19.1 Focus move multiplier — LOCK ×0.5 (R114)
`FOCUS_SPEED_MULT = 0.5`. Held-SHIFT halves the per-frame step on **both** axes: `P_SPEED=5` → **2.5 px/f**
focused. Default confirmed — "twice as slow" is the contract and reads instantly in the hands; not tunable for
v19 (it's a feel constant, not an economy knob). Applies to the move step only (firing/cadence untouched, GDD
§V19.3); PLAY-only + either-SHIFT + held-not-toggle are the Programmer's input handling, not numbers.

## V19.2 Player damage-hitbox radius — LOCK P_HITBOX_R = 6 (R115)
`P_HITBOX_R = 6` px — the **always-on circular damage hitbox** (≈50% of the drawn `P_R=13`; 6/13 = 46%, the
"≈50%, leaning fairer" choice; 6.5 rounded down). The **indicator draws at this same constant** (art_spec
§V19a.2) — one value, no duplicate.

**Collision split (load-bearing — Programmer):** today every player-damage test *and* pickup collection use
`C.P_R` (`combat.py` — boss-ram, asteroid, enemy, ebullet, and step-3 pickup). v19 swaps **only the four
damage tests** to `P_HITBOX_R=6`; **pickup collection stays at `P_R=13`** (GDD §V19.7 decision — pickups stay
generous). **`P_R` stays 13** (unchanged drawn-ship envelope + pickup radius); `P_HITBOX_R=6` is the new
damage circle. The drawn ship is unchanged.

## V19.3 Bullet sizes — LOCK ~1.5×, draw == collision every family (R116)
Honors the artist's invariant (drawn body radius == collision radius per family). All enemy/boss bullets keep
the **single shared collision constant** `EB_R`; player bullet scales with its rect.

| Family | Old draw / collision | **v19 LOCK (draw = collision)** | Δcollision |
|---|---|---|---|
| Enemy aimed RED (+ RED split children) | 5 / 5 | **`EB_R = 8`** | 5→8 (1.6×) |
| HEAVY pellet (GREEN id, purple hue) | 8 / 5 | **`EB_R = 8`** (draw already 8 → visually unchanged) | 5→8 |
| SCOUT streak CYAN | head 4 / 5 | head **`EB_R = 8`**; tail `CYAN_TAIL_LEN = 18` (render-only) | 5→8 |
| Boss fan YELLOW | 6 / 5 | **`EB_R = 8`** | 5→8 |
| NOVA plasma | 6 / 5 | **`EB_R = 8`** | 5→8 |
| Player bullet | rect 4×12 / circle r=PB_H/2=6 | **`PB_W,PB_H = 6,18`** → collision r=PB_H/2 = **9** | 6→9 (1.5×) |

- **`EB_R`: 5 → 8** (1.6× ≈ ~1.5×; 7.5 rounded up to 8). One constant drives every enemy/boss bullet's draw +
  collision; the per-family render-only draw radii (`PELLET_DRAW_R`, `NOVA_BULLET_DRAW_R`, the hardcoded YELLOW
  `6`) are **retired** — each family draws at `EB_R` (art_spec §V19a.3).
- **Pellet decision — LOCK: no larger per-family collision.** The pellet collapses to the shared `EB_R=8`
  (simplest, keeps the single-`EB_R` architecture; its draw was already 8 so it doesn't shrink). Its "fattest
  bullet" read is retired in favour of its **purple `#D230DC` (v17)** identity. (A bigger pellet hitbox would
  buff the splitter for no design reason.)
- **Player bullet `6,18`** — width `PB_W` is draw-only; the circle collision `r=PB_H/2` scales 6→9 with the
  height, so draw + collision stay coupled by construction.
- **`CYAN_TAIL_LEN 12→18`** is a render-only motion streak (×1.5 to track the bigger head); the head dot is the
  collision circle (`EB_R=8`).

## V19.4 No tunneling / no mis-cull — CONFIRMED (R116)
**Tunneling (a bullet skipping the small core between frames): none.** A frame-step only tunnels if it exceeds
`2 × (r_player + r_bullet)`.
- **Enemy/boss bullet × player:** gap = `P_HITBOX_R + EB_R` = 6+8 = **14**; tunnel threshold = **28 px/f**.
  Fastest bullet = SCOUT 7.5 (NOVA lance 6.0, NOVA 5.5, aimed/yellow/child 4.5); worst closing speed with player
  motion (≤5, or 2.5 focused) ≈ **12.5 px/f ≪ 28**. The hit window spans ~28 px of travel → ≥2 sampled frames
  inside it. Safe with wide margin.
- **Player bullet × enemy:** gap vs the *smallest* target (SCOUT r=10) = 9+10 = 19; threshold 38 px/f; player
  bullet ≤ 16 (Railgun) ≪ 38. Safe.
- Speeds are **unchanged** (R110) and every radius **grew** → the hit windows are strictly *wider* than today,
  so v19 makes tunneling **less** likely than the shipped game. No regression.

**Off-screen culling: no mis-cull.** Radii grew by ≤3 px (EB 5→8) / the player rect by 6 px tall (12→18);
center-based edge culling keeps the existing generous off-screen margins, so no in-play bullet is culled early
and no off-screen bullet lingers meaningfully. (Programmer: keep the cull bounds as-is; the few-px-larger body
is immaterial against the 600×800 field.)

## V19.5 Net difficulty + AC13 — leaning fairer, no ramp risk
Effective player-damage radius drops across the board — enemy-bullet hit-sum **18→14** (−22%), ram vs large
asteroid **39→32** — partly offset by ~1.5× bigger incoming bullets; player offense rises slightly (bullet
collision 6→9, easier to land). Net = **easier / fairer**, squarely in design's "about even, leaning fairer"
band (GDD §V19.6). The v1 ramp, spawn rates, caps, and boss cadence are **untouched**, so **AC13 (1–3 min runs)
is not structurally threatened**; the always-smaller hitbox may lengthen expert runs modestly — watch the
balance-probe survival trend post-implementation (the existing parked AC13 lever, no action unless a playtest
confirms a stretch).

## V19.6 Levers (if a playtest retunes)
- **Too easy / runs stretch:** raise `P_HITBOX_R` 6→7 (toward parity, 54% of P_R) and/or `EB_R` 8→9.
- **Too hard:** lower `EB_R` 8→7, or `P_HITBOX_R` stays 6.
- **Focus too strong/weak:** `FOCUS_SPEED_MULT` is a feel contract (0.5); only revisit on an explicit human call.
- All are size knobs; **never** touch the v1 ramp/economy to fix v19 feel.
