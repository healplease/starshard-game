## V18.1 Magnitudes — fire cooldown + bullet speed (LOCK)

Base constants stay; the two new kinds modify them for 480 f, then revert (R31). Resolution is
strongest-wins per stat (GDD §V18.4) — values below are each kind's *target*, not a delta to compound.

| Stat | Base (const) | Overdrive (`O`, cadence) | Railgun (`V`, velocity) | Why |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Fire cooldown** (lower=better) | `FIRE_CD = 12 f` | **6 f** (halved — `=RAPID_CD`, CONFIRM) | **9 f** (cut 3 = "a bit") | OD doubles cadence; RG's 25%-faster bump is the modest ride-along |
| **Bullet speed** (higher=better) | **`PB_SPEED = 10` px/f** (LOCK = the buffable base, R110) | **12 px/f** (+2 = "a bit") | **16 px/f** (+6 = "a lot") | RG's "a lot" (+60%) is 3× OD's "a bit" (+20%) — clear axis separation |

**Railgun cooldown bound CONFIRMED:** `6 < 9 < 12` ✓, and Railgun's cut (3 f) is **smaller** than
Overdrive's halving (6 f) per GDD §V18.4 / R109b.

**Base bullet speed = `PB_SPEED = 10` px/f (LOCK).** This is today's fixed value (config.py:122),
now the single base the buffs modify and the value to revert to on expiry/restart (R110/R31).

**Tunneling is safe at 16 px/f** (R110 watch-item): the smallest player-hittable target is the small
asteroid (`AST_S_R=12` → 24 px diameter); a 16 px/frame step `< 24` px, so a Railgun bullet cannot
skip a target between frames. Larger targets (enemy r=13, bosses r≥60) are never at risk. No collision
or off-screen-cull change — only the per-frame `vy` magnitude grows.

### Cross-stat stacking sanity (both active — derive, don't compound; GDD §V18.4)
- `fire_cd = min(12, 6, 9) = 6 f` (Overdrive wins). `bullet_speed = max(10, 12, 16) = 16 px/f`
  (Railgun wins). Both **bounded** (cd ≥ 6 > 0; speed ≤ 16) and **clean-reverting** (recompute from the
  active set each frame → expiry just drops a contributor). One active → that one. None → 12 f / 10 px/f.

### Why this reweight target (AC13-aware — extends the §V2.3 analysis)
- **Fan 20→12** drops it to the rare tier (= Shield), so it's a **situational grab, not an auto-pick**
  (GDD §V18.2) — matches the brief's "rarer." Combined with the side-beam 2:1 nerf, Fan stops being a

  for the high tail. Repair (low-tail compressor, 30) and Shield (already-trimmed high-tail extender, 12)
  and BOMB (rarest, 6) are **untouched** — no green lever disturbed.

## V18.3 Fan 2:1 center:side cadence — CONFIRMED feel contract (NOT a tunable)
**Confirmed.** Center beam (`0°`) fires at the resolved player cadence; the two flanking beams (`±12°`)
fire **exactly half as often** — one side volley per two center shots. This **2:1 center:side ratio is a
fixed feel contract**, not an economy lever: it is **not** in my tuning levers and must not be re-tuned
for AC13. Geometry (`FAN_ANGLES_DEG=(-12,0,12)`, 3-beam shape, colors) unchanged (R106). The mechanism
(skip-every-other vs. side cooldown) is the Programmer's, as long as it yields exactly 2:1.
