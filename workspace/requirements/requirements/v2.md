## 8. v2 vision (one line)
Occasionally a **bonus pickup** drifts down the starfield; fly over it to collect a buff — patch your
hull or temporarily upgrade your guns or shields — adding a risk/reward "go grab it" beat to each run.

## 9. v2 functional requirements (MoSCoW, testable)

Continues numbering from v1 (R23+). "Shapes + text only / keyboard only / single screen" still applies.
Concrete numbers (durations, spawn rates, drop %, fan count, fire-rate multipliers) are **delegated to
the lead-game-designer (GDD) and level-designer (level_spec)** — this section fixes the *behaviour and
rules*, the design roles fix the *values*.

### MUST have (v2 — the increment is not complete without these)

- **R23 — Bonus pickup entity.** A **bonus object** spawns and **drifts downward with the scroll**
  (like a hazard), drawn as a **shape + a letter/icon** identifying its kind. Flying the ship over it
  (entity–entity collision, same machinery as hazards) **collects** it: the pickup is removed and its
  effect is applied. A bonus that is **not** collected and scrolls off the bottom is removed with **no
  penalty**. At most a small number of bonuses exist on screen at once (entity cap — designer's value).

- **R24 — Repair bonus (instant, one-shot).** Collecting Repair **restores player HP** by a fixed
  amount (designer's value), **clamped to max HP — no overheal**. It is an **instant effect, not a
  timed buff**: it has no duration, no HUD timer, and grants brief collect feedback only (R28).

- **R25 — Spread / fan-fire bonus (timed).** While active, the player weapon fires a **fan of multiple
  beams** (e.g. 3 or 5; designer's value) instead of a single shot. On expiry the weapon **reverts to
  the single forward shot** (R7).

- **R26 — Rapid-fire bonus (timed).** While active, the **fire-rate cooldown is reduced** (faster
  shooting; designer's value). On expiry the fire rate **reverts to the R7 baseline**.

- **R27 — Shield / invulnerability bonus (timed).** While active, the player **takes no damage** from
  asteroids, debris, enemy contact, or enemy bullets. It **reuses the existing i-frame / blink
  visualization (R18)** so the invulnerable state is visible. On expiry the player becomes
  **vulnerable** again. (Distinct from R18's brief post-hit i-frames; this is a longer, pickup-granted
  window — the two may overlap and the player is invulnerable if **either** is active.)

- **R28 — Bonus spawning.** Bonuses enter play via **timed drip** (a bonus appears on a cadence) and/or
  as a **chance drop from destroyed enemies** (a destroyed enemy may leave a bonus). **At least one**
  of these mechanisms must be active in normal play, and the chosen mechanism(s) must be reachable in
  the `--smoke-test` path so QA can verify spawn→collect→apply→expire headlessly. Cadence, drop
  probability, and the kind-selection weighting are the **level-designer's** values.

- **R29 — Active-buff HUD indication.** Each **active timed buff** (R25–R27, plus R32 extras if built)
  is shown on the HUD with an **identifier** (icon/letter/short label) **and a remaining-duration
  indication** (countdown number, shrinking bar, or equivalent) so the player can see what is active
  and roughly how long is left. **Repair (R24)** shows only **brief collect feedback** (e.g. an HP
  flash/popup) and **no persistent timer**. Exact glyphs/colours/layout: Artist + Writer.

- **R30 — Stacking & refresh rules.** Buff interactions must be deterministic and safe:
  - **Different** timed buff *types* (spread, rapid, shield, …) **may be active simultaneously**
    (independent timers).
  - **Re-collecting a timed buff that is already active** **refreshes** its timer (**reset to full
    duration**, not unbounded accumulation; an optional cap is the designer's call). Effects do **not**
    multiply (e.g. two spreads do not become a double-fan).
  - **Repair (R24) never stacks** — it only heals up to max HP (no overheal, no stored charges).
  - No combination of two same-frame pickups may leave the player in a corrupt/contradictory state.

- **R31 — Buff expiry & restart cleanup.** Every timed buff **expires after its duration** and the
  player **cleanly reverts to baseline** (single shot, baseline fire rate, vulnerable). **Restart
  (R13) resets all buff state** — no buff, timer, or pickup leaks from a finished run into the next.

- **R32 — Modular MVC-ish architecture (retire the line cap).** `workspace/game/` is **refactored from
  the single `main.py` into a focused module set** following an MVC-ish separation — e.g.
  `config`/tuning constants, `entities`/models, `systems`/update logic, `view`/render, `input`, and a
  **thin `main.py` entry point** that wires them together. **There is no line cap** (C3 retired). The
  package must stay **importable** and `main.py` must remain the **entry point** (incl. `--smoke-test`).

- **R33 — Smoke gate preserved across the refactor.** After the modular split and the bonus feature,
  `workspace/game/main.py --smoke-test` still **initializes pygame, runs exactly 120 frames with
  simulated input, and exits 0** under `SDL_VIDEODRIVER=dummy` / `SDL_AUDIODRIVER=dummy` (no window,
  no audio, no hang). The smoke run must **exercise at least one full bonus lifecycle** (spawn →
  collect → apply → expire) without raising.

### COULD have (only if cheap and in-scope — designer's discretion, now that there is no line cap)

- **R34 — Score-multiplier bonus (timed).** While active, points earned are multiplied (e.g. ×2);
  shown on the HUD per R29. A natural fit if the buff framework is in place.
- **R35 — Screen-clear / bomb pickup (instant).** Collecting it clears or damages all on-screen
  hazards/enemies once. Higher risk to balance; defer unless trivially cheap.

> Extras (R34/R35) are acceptance-checked **only if implemented**; their absence does not fail v2 as
> long as R23–R33 pass.

## 10. v2 non-goals (still out of scope)
- ❌ Persistent/up-front weapon **selection menus** or an inventory — bonuses are auto-applied on pickup.
- ❌ External icon/image/sound assets for bonuses — **shapes + text only** (C2 still holds).
- ❌ Negative pickups / debuffs, mystery boxes, or pickups that require a key to "use".
- ❌ Carrying buffs **across runs** or saving them to disk (restart fully resets — R31).
- ❌ Mouse/gamepad interaction with bonuses (keyboard-only movement collects them — C1).

## 11. v2 constraints
- **C1, C2, C4, C5, C6 — unchanged** from §6 (single screen, shapes/text only, pygame-ce in `.venv`,
  `--smoke-test` headless, no-hang).
- **C3 — RETIRED.** The ~500-line `main.py` budget is **removed** per the v2 ground rules. Replaced by:
  **modular, focused files** (R32) — optimize for clarity, don't pad; each module earns its place.
- **C7 — Importable package.** Modules under `workspace/game/` import cleanly; `main.py` stays the only
  entry point and keeps the `--smoke-test` contract (R33).

### v2 risks / watch-items
- **State leakage** is the top risk: a buff (or its timer/visual) surviving expiry or restart → flag to
  Programmer; R31 + AC19 exist to catch it.
- **HUD clutter / readability** with multiple active timers → Artist + Writer keep indicators compact.
- **Smoke coverage gap**: if neither spawn path fires within 120 frames the lifecycle goes unverified →
  level-designer must make a bonus reachable in the smoke seed (R28/R33, AC20).
- **Shield vs. R18 i-frames overlap** must be unambiguous (invulnerable if *either* is active) — R27.

## 12. v2 acceptance criteria (what QA will check)

Continues AC numbering (AC14+). **[Smoke]** = headless via `--smoke-test`; **[Play]** = by observation;
**[Code]** = by inspection. The brief's headline AC — *bonuses spawn, collect, apply, expire correctly;
smoke-test still exits 0 after the refactor* — is covered by AC14, AC16, AC20.

- **AC14 [Play]** Bonus objects appear during play (via timed drip and/or enemy drop), drift downward
  with the scroll, and are **collected** when the ship overlaps one; uncollected bonuses leave the
  bottom with no penalty (R23, R28).
- **AC15 [Play]** **Repair** raises HP toward max and **never overheals** past the max (R24).
- **AC16 [Play]** Each timed buff — **spread (R25), rapid (R26), shield (R27)** — **visibly changes
  behaviour while active** (fan of beams / faster fire / no damage taken) and **reverts on expiry**.
- **AC17 [Play]** The HUD shows each **active timed buff** with an identifier **and a remaining-time
  indication**; Repair shows only brief collect feedback, no persistent timer (R29).
- **AC18 [Play]** **Re-collecting** an active timed buff **refreshes its timer** (no unbounded stacking,
  no effect doubling); **different** timed buffs **coexist**; Repair never overheals (R30).
- **AC19 [Play/Smoke]** After **restart (R13)**, no buff, timer, or pickup state carries into the new
  run — the player starts at baseline (R31).
- **AC20 [Smoke]** After the modular refactor, `main.py --smoke-test` **exits 0 after exactly 120
  frames** headless, and the smoke run **spawns, collects, applies, and expires at least one bonus**
  without raising (R28, R33).
- **AC21 [Code]** `workspace/game/` is **modular** (multiple focused MVC-ish modules), the package
  **imports cleanly**, `main.py` is a **thin entry point**, and **no single-file line cap** is enforced
  (R32; C3 retired).

> v2 is complete when **R23–R33 (MUST)** are met and **AC14–AC21** pass. R34/R35 are bonus-only.
> All v1 MUST items (R1–R14) and the v1 smoke gate must **still** pass after the refactor (no regression).
