> acceptance criteria (AC1–AC101) still hold **except** the two base items this increment changes:
> **R3 (player movement)** is amended (precise-mode ×0.5 modifier) and the player's **collision radius**

## V19.1 vision (one line)
A skill expression for dodging: **hold SHIFT to fly at half speed for pixel-precise weaving**, made fair
by a **small circular ship hitbox** (always on, revealed in red while focusing) and rebalanced by
**larger bullets everywhere**.

## V19.2 functional requirements (MoSCoW, testable)

Continues from R113 (R114+). "Shapes + text only / keyboard only / single screen" still applies (C1, C2).
v19 adds an **input modifier**, **changes the player's collision radius**, **scales every projectile's
size**, and **adds one render overlay**; it invents no new system or screen.

### A — Precise mode (amends R3 — player movement)

- **R114 — Hold SHIFT = precise mode (PLAY only); ship move speed halved while held.** While **either
  SHIFT key is held** during **PLAY**, the player's per-frame movement is **halved (×0.5)** on both axes
  ("twice as slow as normal"), enabling precise dodging. It is a **held modifier**, not a toggle:
  releasing SHIFT **immediately restores** full move speed; it persists across frames only as long as the
  key is down. Precise mode **does not affect firing** (cadence, bullet count, geometry, or speed are
  unchanged), **does not change the hitbox size** (the hitbox is the same small circle whether or not
  SHIFT is held — R115), and applies **only in PLAY** (it has no effect in START/PAUSE/STATS/GAME_OVER).
  This **amends R3** (which moved the ship at a single fixed `P_SPEED`). The exact multiplier is the
  Level-designer's (default **×0.5**).

### B — Circular player hitbox, always smaller than the ship (amends R16–R18 collision radius)

- **R115 — The player's damage hitbox is ALWAYS a smaller circle, decoupled from the drawn ship.** The
  player's **collision shape for taking damage** is a **circle whose radius is ~50% of the ship's drawn
  size** (i.e. roughly half today's `P_R=13`). This hitbox is **always active** — **not** only during
  precise mode (confirmed with the human 2026-06-07); SHIFT only *reveals* it (R117), it does not resize
  it. The change applies to **every player-damage collision** today computed against `P_R`: asteroid/debris
  ram (R16/R17), enemy ram, enemy/boss **bullet** hits, and **boss body ram** — each now tests the new
  smaller radius. The **drawn ship is unchanged** (same sprite shapes/size as v1): only the *hitbox*
  shrinks, so the visible ship is larger than its true hitbox. The exact hitbox radius (a concrete px
  ≈ 50% of `P_R`) is the Level-designer's. *(See §V19.3: whether **bonus-pickup collection** — also
  computed against `P_R` today — shrinks with the damage hitbox or stays generous is a delegated design
  call; default: pickup generosity **unchanged**, only the *damage* hitbox shrinks.)*

### C — Larger bullets, every family (balance for the smaller hitbox)

- **R116 — All bullets are ~50% larger, in BOTH draw and collision size.** Every projectile family —
  **player bullets and all enemy/boss bullets** (player shots, enemy aimed bullets `EB_R`, GREEN pellets,
  RED children, NOVA bullets, boss bullets) — is enlarged by **~50%** in **both its drawn size and its
  collision size**, so the visible bullet and its hitbox stay matched. This is the **balancing lever** for
  the shrunken player hitbox (R115). The per-family magnitudes (the concrete ~1.5× sizes / radii for each
  bullet type, draw and collision) are the Level-designer's; enlarging bullets must not break bullet
  motion, spawn positions, or off-screen culling (no R6/R7 regression), and must not introduce tunneling
  (a bullet skipping a target between frames). *(Bullet **speeds** are unchanged — v19 changes size, not
  velocity; the v18 buffable-speed contract R110 is untouched.)*

### D — Precise-mode hitbox indicator (render-only)

- **R117 — While SHIFT is held, show the player's hitbox as a red circle at 50% opacity.** During **PLAY**,
  **while (and only while) SHIFT is held**, draw the player's **actual hitbox** as a **red circle at ~50%
  opacity**, centered on the ship at the **true hitbox radius (R115)**. It is **not shown** when SHIFT is
  released, nor outside PLAY. This is **render-only**: it must **not** change the hitbox size, movement, or
  any collision (it is purely a readout of R115). The exact red hue, alpha (≈50%), blend mode, and render
  slot are the Artist's.

### E — Enemy hitboxes unchanged

- **R118 — Enemy / asteroid / boss collision radii are unchanged.** Only the **player's** damage hitbox
  changes (R115). The collision radii of asteroids (`a.r`), enemies (`e.r`), and bosses (`boss.r`) — i.e.
  the *bodies* the player rams — are **unchanged**. (Their **bullets** grow per R116, but that is the
  projectile-size change, not an enemy-body change.) Enemy/boss firing, movement, HP, and spawn behavior
  are untouched.

### F — Smoke + tests (MUST)

- **R119 — Smoke gate green, precise mode + hitbox exercised, suite grows, no regression.** After v19,
  `main.py --smoke-test` still **exits 0 after exactly 120 frames** headless (R14 preserved) with **no
  AC1–AC101 regression**. The smoke/test path **MUST exercise** precise mode (a SHIFT-held movement frame
  proving the ×0.5 speed) and the **shrunken player hitbox** (a hit that lands inside the old `P_R` but
  outside the new radius no longer damages). The **pytest suite stays green and grows** with checks for:
  SHIFT halving movement and reverting on release (R114), the smaller circular hitbox always-on (R115),
  all-bullets-~50%-larger in draw+collision (R116), enemy-body radii unchanged (R118), and (by render
  smoke) the SHIFT-only red indicator (R117) — while AC1–AC101 still hold.

> v19 is acceptance-checked against R114–R119 (all MUST). No COULD items this increment.

## V19.4 non-goals (still out of scope)
- ❌ Changing the **drawn ship** — its shapes, size, and colors are unchanged; only the *hitbox* shrinks.
- ❌ Changing **enemy/asteroid/boss collision radii** (R118), or any enemy/boss firing/movement/HP/spawn.
- ❌ Changing **bullet speeds** or the v18 buffable-speed contract (R110) — v19 changes bullet **size**
  only.
- ❌ Making precise mode a **toggle**, giving it a cooldown/meter, or letting it affect **firing** or the
  **hitbox size** — SHIFT only **slows movement** and **reveals** the (already-small) hitbox.
- ❌ Showing the red hitbox circle **outside precise mode** or outside **PLAY** (R117).
- ❌ **Normalizing diagonal movement speed**, new screens, persistence, or external assets/audio (C1/C2).

## V19.5 risks / watch-items
- **Hitbox vs draw size conflated.** Today `P_R` is used for *both* drawing-adjacent sizing and collision;
  shrinking it naively would shrink the visible ship. → R115 makes the **draw size unchanged** explicit;
  the Programmer must split the **draw constant** from the **collision radius**.
- **"All bullets larger" missing a family.** Several bullet families exist (player, `EB_R`, pellet, child,
  NOVA, boss) with separate draw/collision constants. → R116 says **every** family, **draw AND collision**;
  AC checks more than one family.
- **Bigger/changed collision → tunneling or false culling.** Larger bullet radii are safe, but the change
  must not let a bullet skip a target between frames or mis-cull. → R116 flags it; QA re-checks.
- **Indicator leaks into collision.** The red circle is a readout only. → R117 forbids it changing any
  collision/movement; AC verifies the hitbox behaves identically with SHIFT up vs down (only speed +
  overlay differ).
- **Precise mode stuck on / wrong state.** A held modifier must clear the instant SHIFT releases and never
  fire outside PLAY. → R114; AC checks revert-on-release and PLAY-only.
- **Pickup feel regression.** If the pickup radius silently shrinks with the damage hitbox, bonuses get
  noticeably harder to grab. → flagged as a delegated decision (§V19.3), default keep generous.

## V19.6 acceptance criteria (AC102–AC108)

Continues AC numbering (AC102+). **[Smoke]** = headless via `--smoke-test`; **[Play]** = by observation;
**[Code]** = by inspection.

- **AC102 [Play/Code]** Holding **SHIFT** during PLAY **halves** the ship's per-frame movement on both
  axes; **releasing** SHIFT restores full speed the same frame. Precise mode does **not** change firing
  and has **no effect** outside PLAY (R114).
- **AC103 [Code]** The player's **damage hitbox is a circle of radius ≈ 50% of the ship's drawn size**,
  **always active regardless of SHIFT**, used by every player-damage collision (asteroid/enemy/bullet ram
  + boss ram); the **drawn ship size is unchanged** (R115).
- **AC104 [Play/Code]** The smaller hitbox **measurably reduces** damage collisions: a hazard/bullet
  position that **would have hit at the old `P_R`** but lies **outside the new radius** **no longer
  damages** the player (R115).
- **AC105 [Code]** **Every** bullet family is **~50% larger** in **both** its drawn size and its collision
  size (player shots + at least the enemy `EB_R` family verified; draw and collision stay matched) (R116).
- **AC106 [Play]** While SHIFT is held in PLAY, a **red circle at ~50% opacity** is drawn on the ship at
  the **true hitbox radius**; it is **absent** when SHIFT is released and outside PLAY, and toggling it
  changes **no** collision/movement (R117).
- **AC107 [Code]** Asteroid, enemy, and boss **collision radii are unchanged**; only the player hitbox and
  bullet sizes changed (R118).
- **AC108 [Smoke]** `main.py --smoke-test` **exits 0 after exactly 120 frames** headless while exercising
  precise mode (SHIFT-held ×0.5 movement) and the shrunken hitbox; the **pytest suite is green and has
  grown** with checks for R114–R118; **no AC1–AC101 regression** (R119).

> v19 is complete when **R114–R119 (MUST)** are met and **AC102–AC108** pass, with **no regression** of
> AC1–AC101 (smoke + pytest green). The load-bearing gates are the **always-on smaller circular hitbox**
> (R115/AC103–AC104) and the **SHIFT ×0.5 + red-indicator** behaviour (R114/R117, AC102/AC106).
