## V18.1 vision (one line)
Fan stops being a no-brainer (its flanking beams now fire half as often, and it drops less often), and
the old Rapid pickup is split into **two distinct fire/speed buffs** — one cadence-leaning, one
velocity-leaning — that share Rapid's old slot in the drop table.

## V18.2 functional requirements (MoSCoW, testable)

Continues from R105 (R106+). "Shapes + text only / keyboard only / single screen" still applies (C1, C2).
v18 reuses the entire v2 bonus framework — it adds **two new BonusKinds + a Fan firing-rate rule**,
removes one BonusKind, and **reweights the existing RNG ladder**; it invents no new bonus system.

### A — Fan nerf (amends v2 R25 — Fan/spread bonus)

- **R106 — Fan side-beams fire at HALF the center cadence (2:1 center:side).** While Fan is active, the
  **center beam (`0°`) is unchanged** — it fires at the player's current fire cadence (R7, as modified by
  any active fire-rate buff). The **two flanking beams (`±12°`)** fire **once for every two center-beam
  shots** — i.e. the side beams' firing rate is exactly **half** the center beam's ("twice as slow as the
  main beam"). The 2:1 **center:side rate ratio is the contract**; the exact mechanism (skip every other
  center shot for the sides, vs. a separate side-beam cooldown) is the Programmer's. Fan **geometry**
  (the `±12°` angles, the 3-beam shape, beam colors/visuals) is **unchanged** — only the side beams'
  *cadence* changes. This **amends R25** (which had all beams fire at the main cadence); on Fan expiry the
  weapon still reverts to the single forward shot (R25/R7).

- **R107 — Fan is rarer (lower RNG weight).** Fan's bonus-selection weight is **reduced below its current
  value (20)**; Fan drops **strictly less often** than before. The **exact new Fan weight** and **where
  the freed weight goes** are the **Level-designer's** call, subject to R111 (the full ladder still sums
  to 100). This amends only R28's *weighting* of Fan, not the spawn mechanism.

### B — Rapid removed; two new fire/speed bonus kinds replace it (amends/retires v2 R26)

- **R108 — Rapid bonus RETIRED.** The **Rapid `BonusKind` is removed entirely** — its registry entry,
  effect, RNG-ladder slot, HUD pill, and color are deleted; nothing references a `RAPID` kind after v18.
  **R26 (rapid-fire bonus) is RETIRED**, superseded by R109. The smoke seed that forced a Rapid
  (`SMOKE_BONUS_KIND = "RAPID"`) MUST be repointed (R112).

- **R109 — Two new timed fire/speed BonusKinds (new content).** Two **distinct** new bonus kinds join the
  roster, each a **timed buff** that **mirrors the v2 timed-buff shape verbatim**: it shows on the HUD with
  an identifier **and** a remaining-duration indication (R29); **re-collecting the same kind refreshes its
  timer** and does **not** multiply effects (R30); it **expires after its duration and reverts the player
  to baseline**, and **restart resets all of it** (R31). Buff **durations** are the Designer's call
  (default: Rapid's old 480 f) unless the Designer decides otherwise. The two kinds' **effects** are:
  - **(a) the "fire-rate" kind:** **fire-rate up by the current Rapid amount** — the fire cooldown is
    **halved** exactly as the old Rapid did (`RAPID_CD = FIRE_CD // 2`, 12 f→6 f) — **PLUS bullet speed up
    *a bit*** (a small increase on the baseline bullet speed; magnitude = Level-designer).
  - **(b) the "velocity" kind:** **bullet speed up *a lot*** (a large increase on the baseline bullet
    speed; magnitude = Level-designer) **PLUS fire rate up *a bit*** (a modest cooldown reduction, smaller
    than the (a)/Rapid halving; magnitude = Level-designer). Net feel: faster bullets make aiming easier
    with only a light cadence bump.
  - Both kinds are **distinct from each other and from every other bonus** in HUD letter + color (Artist)
    and name (Writer); one MAY reuse Rapid's old cyan but the two MUST be **visually distinguishable** from
    each other and the rest of the bonus palette.

- **R110 — Bullet speed becomes a buffable player stat.** The player's bullet speed has a **single base
  constant** (today fixed); R109(a)/(b) modify it for the buff's duration. On expiry (and on restart,
  R31) bullet speed **reverts to the base constant**. The base value and the "a bit"/"a lot" deltas are
  the Level-designer's (§V18.3). Changing bullet speed must not break existing bullet behavior, collision,
  or off-screen culling (no regression of R6/R7/AC-base).

- **R111 — Weight-sharing contract: the two new kinds split Rapid's old weight; ladder sums to 100.**
  The **combined** RNG-selection weight of the two new kinds (R109a + R109b) **equals the old Rapid
  weight (20)** — together they occupy exactly the slot Rapid vacated. The **split between the two** is the
  Level-designer's call (**default: even, 10/10**). Combined with R107 (Fan's reduced weight redistributed
  elsewhere), **the full bonus-selection ladder MUST still sum to 100** with **every kind's slot
  non-overlapping** (the roll-0–99 ladder stays exhaustive and exclusive). The roster after v18 is:
  **Repair · Fan · new-fire-rate-kind · new-velocity-kind · Shield · Score×2 · Bomb** (Rapid gone, two new
  in, 7 kinds total). Exact per-kind weights + ladder ordering = Level-designer.

### C — Stacking across shared stats (clarifies R30 for the new overlap)

- **R112 — Deterministic resolution when buffs touch the same stat.** v18 introduces buffs that **both**
  modify the **fire cooldown** (Fan-affected center cadence, the new fire-rate kind, the new velocity
  kind's small bump) and **bullet speed** (both new kinds). Per R30, **different** timed-buff types may be
  **active simultaneously** with independent timers — so the new fire-rate and velocity kinds **can be
  active at once**, each touching fire rate *and* bullet speed. The combined effect on each shared stat
  **MUST be deterministic, bounded, and revert cleanly on each buff's independent expiry** (no corrupt /
  runaway / negative-cooldown state — R30/R31). The **resolution rule per stat** (e.g. apply the strongest
  single active modifier — recommended default — vs. compose them) is the **Designer's** call; whatever is
  chosen MUST be specified so QA can verify it and MUST never produce a non-positive cooldown or a stuck
  buffed stat after expiry.

### D — Smoke + tests (MUST)

- **R113 — Smoke gate green, new bonuses exercised headlessly, suite grows.** After v18,
  `main.py --smoke-test` still **exits 0 after exactly 120 frames** headless (R14/R33 preserved) with **no
  AC1–AC93 regression**. The smoke seed **MUST no longer reference `RAPID`** (R108) and MUST seed a
  **valid post-v18 kind** so the smoke run still exercises a full bonus lifecycle (spawn→collect→apply→
  expire, R33/AC20) — ideally one of the two **new** kinds, so its effect path runs headlessly. The
  **pytest suite stays green and grows** with checks for: Fan's 2:1 side cadence (R106), Fan's reduced
  weight (R107), Rapid's removal (R108), each new kind's fire-rate + bullet-speed effect (R109/R110), the
  combined-weight-=-20 + **ladder-sums-100** contract (R111), and clean stacking/expiry (R112) — while
  AC14–AC21 (the bonus framework) and all AC1–AC93 still hold.

> v18 is acceptance-checked against R106–R113 (all MUST). No COULD items this increment.

## V18.4 non-goals (still out of scope)
- ❌ Changing **Fan geometry** — the `±12°` angles, the 3-beam shape, and Fan's colors are **unchanged**;
  only the side-beam **cadence** (R106) and Fan's **drop weight** (R107) change.
- ❌ Touching any **other bonus** — Repair (R24), Shield (R27), Score×2 (R34), Bomb (R35) are unchanged in
  effect, weight semantics aside (their RNG weights may shift only as part of R111's resum-to-100).
- ❌ A **third** new bonus, debuffs, or negative pickups — exactly **two** kinds replace Rapid (R109).
- ❌ **Carrying buffs across runs** or saving the new stats to disk — restart fully resets (R31).
- ❌ Changing the **bonus spawn mechanism** (R28) or HUD framework (R29) — only Fan's weight + the
  ladder's membership/weights change; pills still render via the existing R29 machinery.
- ❌ External assets / audio — the new pills are **shapes + a letter** (C2); keyboard-only (C1).

## V18.5 risks / watch-items
- **Ladder no longer sums to 100.** Removing Rapid, adding two kinds, and lowering Fan all touch the
  roll-0–99 table; an off-by-one leaves a gap or overlap → a kind never/always drops. → R111 makes
  **sums-to-100 + non-overlapping** a hard contract; AC checks it explicitly.
- **2:1 read as "side beams just slower."** R106 is a **firing-rate** ratio (sides fire half as *often*),
  not a bullet-speed or angle change. → Programmer must hit exactly 2:1 center:side; AC measures shot
  counts, not visuals.
- **Negative / runaway cooldown from stacking.** Fire-rate kind + velocity kind both cut the cooldown and
  could compound to ≤0, or a buffed stat could stick after expiry. → R112 forces a bounded, deterministic,
  clean-reverting resolution; AC re-checks baseline restoration on expiry.
- **Dangling `RAPID` references.** R108 removes the kind; a leftover reference (smoke seed, HUD map, test)
  crashes or silently keeps Rapid alive. → R108 + R113 require full removal + the smoke seed repointed;
  AC checks no `RAPID` kind remains.
- **Bullet-speed change breaks culling/collision.** Faster bullets must still cull off-screen and collide
  correctly (no tunneling/regression). → R110 flags it; QA re-checks base bullet behavior.

## V18.6 acceptance criteria (AC94–AC101)

Continues AC numbering (AC94+). **[Smoke]** = headless via `--smoke-test`; **[Play]** = by observation;
**[Code]** = by inspection.

- **AC94 [Play/Code]** While **Fan** is active, the **center beam** fires at the player's current cadence
  and the **two side beams (`±12°`) fire half as often** — exactly **one side volley per two center
  shots** (2:1 center:side). Fan's angles/shape/colors are unchanged (R106).
- **AC95 [Code]** **Fan's RNG weight is lower than its previous value (20)** — Fan drops strictly less
  often than before (R107).
- **AC96 [Code]** The **Rapid bonus is gone** — no `RAPID` `BonusKind`, effect, ladder slot, HUD pill, or
  color remains anywhere (incl. the smoke seed) (R108).
- **AC97 [Play/Code]** The new **fire-rate kind** halves the fire cooldown (the old Rapid amount,
  12 f→6 f) **and** increases bullet speed by a small amount; both revert on expiry (R109a, R110).
- **AC98 [Play/Code]** The new **velocity kind** increases bullet speed by a **large** amount **and** the
  fire cooldown by a **small** amount (smaller cut than the fire-rate kind); both revert on expiry
  (R109b, R110).
- **AC99 [Code]** The two new kinds' **combined RNG weight equals 20** (the old Rapid weight), and the
  **full bonus ladder sums to 100** with **non-overlapping** slots across all **7** kinds (R111).
- **AC100 [Play/Smoke]** Both new kinds behave as **timed buffs**: each shows a HUD pill **with a
  remaining-duration indication** (R29); **re-collecting refreshes** the timer without doubling the effect
  (R30); on **expiry** the player reverts to baseline fire rate + bullet speed; and **restart** clears all
  buff/timer/stat state (R31). When both new kinds are active at once, the effect on each shared stat is
  **deterministic, bounded, and reverts cleanly** per the recorded resolution rule (R112).
- **AC101 [Smoke]** `main.py --smoke-test` **exits 0 after exactly 120 frames** headless with the smoke
  seed repointed off `RAPID` to a valid post-v18 kind that still exercises a full bonus lifecycle; the
  **pytest suite is green and has grown** with checks for R106–R112; **no AC1–AC93 regression** (R113).

> v18 is complete when **R106–R113 (MUST)** are met and **AC94–AC101** pass, with **no regression** of
> AC1–AC93 (smoke + pytest green). The weight-sharing **ladder-sums-to-100** contract (R111/AC99) and the
> Fan **2:1 side cadence** (R106/AC94) are the load-bearing gates.
