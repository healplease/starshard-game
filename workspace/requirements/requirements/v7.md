## 24. v7 vision (one line)
Every so often the normal run pauses: the field is swept clean and a huge, slow **Mothership** glides in
to the centre of the screen, oscillating side to side while it cycles a learnable attack pattern — survive
its big health pool and you bank a **big point reward** and the normal run resumes, until the next boss.

## 25. v7 functional requirements (MoSCoW, testable)

Continues numbering from v6 (R56+). "Shapes + text only / keyboard only / single screen" still applies
(C1, C2). The bosses **reuse existing machinery** wherever possible — the **v6 flush + flash** for the
arrival field-clear, the **v5 enemy roster** for the minions, and the **v5 green→red split** pattern for
attack 4 — so this increment adds an **encounter manager + a Boss entity**, not new core systems. Concrete
numbers (breakpoint metric/value, boss HP/size/speed/oscillation, moveset cadence, fan/split counts &
angles, reward, damage) are **delegated** — see §26. Examples below are illustrative only.

### A — The boss-fight loop (MUST)

- **R56 — Boss breakpoint trigger (periodic).** A boss encounter begins automatically when a **periodic
  breakpoint** is reached during active play. The breakpoint is **time-based OR point-based OR both**, and
  whether it is a **fixed interval** or **scales per boss** — the **metric and the exact value(s)** are a
  **Designer/Level-designer value** (§26). The breakpoint **repeats**: after one boss is resolved and the
  normal loop resumes (R62), the *next* breakpoint brings the *next* boss.
  - **⚠ AC13 RECONCILIATION — the #1 thing to resolve (DELEGATED, flagged, not assumed):** AC13 holds that
    a typical run lasts **1–3 minutes**. The human's example breakpoints (*every 5 min* / *every 5000 pts*)
    would mean **most runs never reach a boss** — the headline feature would rarely be seen. The
    Designer/Level-designer **MUST** consciously choose one of: **(a)** lower the breakpoint so a boss
    reliably appears inside a 1–3 min run (e.g. a first boss at a smaller time/score), **(b)** accept bosses
    as a deliberate **late/expert-run** event and document that most runs end before one, or **(c)** re-tune
    the ramp/AC13 band for this game's new pacing. **This decision drives the whole increment** and must be
    recorded in the GDD + level_spec. (See §28.)

- **R57 — Boss-arrival field-clear (system flush + flash; NO charge, NO score).** The instant a boss
  encounter begins, the field is **cleared** by **reusing the v6 bomb flush + flash** (GDD §V6.3/§V6.5) so
  it reads identically to a player bomb — **all enemies, all asteroids/debris, and all enemy projectiles
  (incl. in-flight green pellets + already-split red children) are removed**, while **player bullets, all
  pickups, and cosmetics (particles/starfield) are spared** (exactly the v6 §R48 scope). **BA rulings (NOT
  delegated):** this clear is **system-initiated, not a player action**, so it **consumes NO bomb charge**
  (the player's charge count is untouched) and it **awards NO score** (silent despawn, like the v6 flush).
  The Programmer **factors** the v6 flush/flash so the encounter manager can trigger it **without** the
  charge-decrement / lockout path. (The boss itself enters *after* the clear, so it is not flushed by its
  own arrival.)

- **R58 — Spawn freeze while the boss is alive.** From the moment a boss encounter begins until the boss is
  defeated (R62), the **normal spawners are frozen**: the **v1 asteroid/debris spawner** and the **v5 enemy
  spawner** produce **nothing**. The **only** new hostiles that may appear are the **boss's own moveset
  minions** (R67). On boss defeat the freeze **lifts** and normal spawning **resumes** (R62). Whether the
  **v2 bonus-pickup drip** is also frozen during the fight, and whether boss minions are eligible for
  pickup drops, are **Level-designer values** (§26); BA default-recommendation (non-binding): freeze the
  bonus drip too so the fight is a clean, self-contained arena.

- **R59 — Boss entrance + central oscillation.** The boss **enters slowly** from off-screen (top), travels
  in, and **settles at the vertical centre** of the screen, after which it **moves slowly left and right
  (oscillates)** to make its position harder to predict and its shots harder to dodge. The **entrance path
  + speed**, the **resting vertical position**, and the **oscillation amplitude + speed** are **Designer
  values** (§26). Whether the boss may begin attacking (R66) **during** its entrance or only **after** it
  settles is a **Designer value** (§26).

- **R60 — Boss health pool + the boss is hittable.** The boss has a **large health pool** (it takes many
  player-bullet hits to destroy — far more than any v5 enemy). The exact HP (in player-bullet hits) is a
  **Designer value** (§26). **BA ruling (NOT delegated):** the **player's weapon always damages the boss** —
  Z fire (incl. v2 Rapid / Fan modifiers) hits it via the normal player-bullet × enemy collision path, so
  the boss is **always defeatable**; it is never invulnerable to player shots. Player bullets are **consumed
  on hit** as usual.

- **R61 — Boss damage to the player (ram + bullets).** The boss can damage the player **two** ways: by
  **ram / body contact** (the player colliding with the boss body costs HP) and by its **moveset bullets**
  (R68; the yellow fan + red split children deal enemy-bullet damage). Both route through the existing
  §V2.7 player-damage step (first-hit-wins, honouring i-frames R18 / shield R27). The **boss ram-damage
  value** and the **boss-bullet damage value** are **Designer values** (§26) — bullet damage may simply
  reuse the flat enemy-bullet damage (`EB_DMG`).

- **R62 — Boss defeat reward + normal-loop resume.** When the boss's health reaches 0 it is **destroyed**
  and the player is awarded a **big flat point reward** (clearly larger than any normal kill; the exact
  value is a **Designer/Level-designer value**, §26). On defeat the **spawn freeze lifts (R58)** and the
  **normal loop resumes** — asteroids + v5 enemies spawn again under the usual ramp — **until the next
  breakpoint (R56)** brings the next boss. Any boss minions still alive at the moment of defeat **persist**
  as normal enemies (BA ruling — they are ordinary v5 enemies once spawned); whether the kill also clears
  them is a **Designer** call (§26).

- **R63 — Bomb-vs-boss rule (player bombs mid-fight).** The player's **bomb (X) remains usable during a
  boss fight** (R46 unchanged) and **costs a charge as normal** (it IS a player action, unlike the R57
  system clear). **BA ruling (NOT delegated):** a player bomb during the fight **clears the boss's minions
  and all boss/enemy bullets** (consistent with the R48 flush scope) — it is a legitimate "clear the adds +
  panic the bullet-wall" tool. **DELEGATED (§26):** whether the flush **also damages the boss itself** —
  recommended default **boss is IMMUNE to the bomb flush** (you cannot bomb-kill a boss; the health pool
  must be earned with shots), with an optional **flat chip** as a Designer balance lever. Whatever the rule,
  it must be **deterministic** and stated in the GDD.

- **R64 — Boss HUD (health bar + label).** While a boss is active, the HUD shows a **boss health bar** that
  tracks the boss's current health, plus a **boss label/name** (e.g. "MOTHERSHIP"). It must be **visually
  distinct** from the player HP bar (R10) and from the v2 buff pills (R29) and not collide with them. The
  bar's **placement, size, colors** are the **Artist's**, and the **label text** is the **Writer's** (§26).
  It appears when the encounter begins and is removed on defeat.

- **R65 — Smoke gate preserved + a boss exercised headlessly.** After v7, `workspace/game/main.py
  --smoke-test` still **initializes pygame, runs exactly 120 frames with simulated input, and exits 0**
  under `SDL_VIDEODRIVER=dummy` / `SDL_AUDIODRIVER=dummy` (no window, no audio, no hang) — R14/R33/R43/R54
  preserved, and **no AC1–AC38 regression**. Additionally, because a natural 120-frame run will not reach
  any realistic breakpoint, the smoke path **must seed/force a boss encounter early** so that, within the
  120 frames, the run exercises **the arrival field-clear (R57), the boss entrance (R59), and at least one
  moveset attack step (R66/R67/R68)** — all **without raising**. The seed mechanism (a forced breakpoint, a
  pre-spawned boss, a `SMOKE_BOSS_*` hook, etc.) is the **Programmer's choice** (mirroring the v5
  `SMOKE_SPLIT_*` and v6 `SMOKE_BOMB_*` seeds); the **requirement** is that the boss loop actually runs in
  `--smoke-test`.

### B — The first boss: the MOTHERSHIP (MUST)

- **R66 — Mothership identity + repeating 4-step moveset.** The first boss is the **Mothership**: a
  **large, slow** boss that follows R59–R64 (central entrance, oscillation, large HP, boss HUD). It runs a
  **moveset of exactly 4 attack steps** (R67 steps 1–3 + R68 step 4) that **repeat in a fixed cycle**
  (1 → 2 → 3 → 4 → 1 → …) until it is defeated. The **order, the cadence (the interval between steps), and
  the loop behaviour** are **Designer values** (§26); R66 only fixes that there are these 4 steps and that
  they **repeat** until defeat.

- **R67 — Moveset steps 1–3: spawn waves of the v5 enemy types.** The Mothership's first three attack steps
  **spawn reinforcements that are exactly the v5 enemy kinds** (GDD §V5.2), in these counts:
  - **Step 1 — spawn 5 REGULAR enemies.**
  - **Step 2 — spawn 2 HEAVY enemies.**
  - **Step 3 — spawn 7 SCOUT enemies.**
  These minions **are ordinary v5 enemies** once spawned — they move, fire (REGULAR red / HEAVY green
  splitting pellet / SCOUT cyan), take damage, and are destroyed exactly as in v5 (R36–R40), and they appear
  **even though the normal enemy spawner is frozen** (R58 — they come from the boss, not the spawner). The
  **spawn positions/timing within the step** are a **Designer value**; whether minions **award normal score
  / are eligible for pickup drops**, and the **minion cap rule** (e.g. does step 1 re-spawn 5 if the
  previous wave is still alive?) are **Designer/Level-designer values** (§26).

- **R68 — Moveset step 4: yellow fan that midway splits into 12 red bullets.** The Mothership's fourth
  attack step **fires a fan of YELLOW bullets** that, **partway across the screen ("midway")**, **each split
  into RED bullets** — **12 red bullets total** flying outward **in all directions** (an even ~360° burst,
  ≈30° apart). This **reuses the v5 green→red splitting-pellet machinery** (GDD §V5.4): a frozen
  split-distance/timer along each bullet's heading, then a removal-and-replace into red children that are
  **ordinary RED enemy bullets** (flat `EB_DMG` damage, despawn off-screen, **never split again** — no
  recursion). **BA ruling (NOT delegated):** the children are plain red enemy bullets reusing the existing
  enemy-bullet path (as in v5). **DELEGATED (§26):** the **yellow-fan count + spread**, the exact **split
  trigger** ("midway" = screen midpoint, or after N frames/distance — recommend the v5 frozen-distance
  approach), the **12 red children's exact directions** (even 360° / ~30° apart) and their **speed +
  damage** — all **Designer values**, with the brief's "**12 red, all directions**" fixed as the target.

## 27. v7 non-goals (still out of scope)
- ❌ Multiple simultaneous bosses, or a selectable boss roster — there is **one** boss active at a time;
  the Mothership is the **first and only** boss this increment (more bosses are a *later* increment).
- ❌ Boss phases / enrage / health-gated form changes — the Mothership has **one** health pool and **one**
  repeating moveset (R66); no second-phase transformation this scope.
- ❌ Boss-specific loot tables, drops, or unique pickups beyond the existing v2/v6 economy.
- ❌ A separate boss "arena", background change, scrolling stop, or camera move — same single screen, same
  scrolling starfield; only the spawners freeze (R58).
- ❌ Recursive / multi-stage splitting on attack-4 — the 12 red children are **terminal** (no re-split),
  exactly like the v5 red children.
- ❌ The system arrival field-clear (R57) consuming a player charge or awarding score — it is free and
  silent (it is the system, not the player).
- ❌ External assets — boss, health bar, and bullets are **shapes + text** only (C2); no audio (C2).
- ❌ Mouse / gamepad — keyboard-only (C1); controls are unchanged from v6 (Z fire / X bomb).

## 28. v7 risks / watch-items
- **★ Breakpoint vs AC13 (the headline risk).** A 5-min/5000-pt gate against 1–3 min runs means the boss —
  the whole feature — is **rarely or never seen**. This is **flagged for the Designer/Level-designer to
  resolve explicitly** (R56 / §26 top row): lower the gate, accept a late-run event, or re-tune AC13. **Do
  not ship a boss that no normal run ever reaches.** This also interacts with run length: a boss fight
  *itself* takes time and could push runs **past** the 3-min ceiling — the chosen cadence + boss HP + reward
  must be balanced so the loop stays within (or deliberately redefines) the AC13 band.
- **Spawn-freeze leaks / fails to resume.** If the freeze (R58) doesn't fully gate the v1 asteroid + v5
  enemy spawners, the boss arena gets polluted; if the freeze doesn't **lift** on defeat (R62), the normal
  game never comes back → AC41 checks both the freeze and the resume.
- **Unwinnable / unkillable boss.** If player shots don't reach the boss (R60) or the HP pool is mis-tuned
  huge, the fight stalls; if the boss never settles/oscillates (R59) it may sit off-screen → AC42/AC43.
- **Attack-4 split correctness.** The yellow→red split must produce **exactly 12** red children in an even
  all-directions burst and **not** recurse — reusing the v5 frozen-split avoids the "homing/0-distance
  split" failure mode (GDD §V5.4 §16) → AC50.
- **Bomb-vs-boss ambiguity.** Players *will* bomb during a boss fight; the rule (R63) must be deterministic
  (minions+bullets cleared, boss immune-or-chipped per the Designer) so a bomb can't trivially delete a
  boss nor be a dead no-op → AC46.
- **HUD collision.** The boss health bar (R64) must not overlap the player HP bar / buff pills / bomb
  readout → Artist places it clear; AC47.
- **Smoke coverage gap.** A natural 120-f run reaches no breakpoint, so the boss loop would go unverified →
  R65 requires the smoke path to **seed a boss** so arrival-clear + entrance + ≥1 attack fire in-budget →
  AC51. The seed must also not regress the v5 split / v6 bomb seeds already in the smoke run.

## 29. v7 acceptance criteria (what QA will check)

Continues AC numbering (AC39+). **[Smoke]** = headless via `--smoke-test`; **[Play]** = by observation;
**[Code]** = by inspection. The brief's headline — *on a periodic breakpoint the field clears (free, no
charge) and normal spawns freeze; a slow boss enters, holds centre oscillating, has a big HP pool +
moveset; on defeat → big points + resume; the Mothership cycles spawn-5-regular / 2-heavy / 7-scout /
yellow-fan-→-12-red* — is covered by AC39–AC51.

- **AC39 [Play/Smoke]** When the configured **breakpoint** (R56, the Designer's metric+value) is reached, a
  **boss encounter begins** automatically; it **repeats** on subsequent breakpoints after each boss is
  resolved (R56).
- **AC40 [Play/Smoke]** On boss arrival the **field is cleared** by the v6 flush+flash visual — **all
  enemies, asteroids, and enemy bullets (incl. green pellets + split red children) are removed**, while
  **player bullets, all pickups, and cosmetics survive** — and this clear **consumes NO bomb charge** (the
  player's charge count is unchanged) and **awards NO score** (R57).
- **AC41 [Play/Smoke]** While the boss is alive, the **v1 asteroid spawner and the v5 enemy spawner produce
  nothing** (the only new hostiles are boss minions, R67); on **boss defeat the freeze lifts** and normal
  asteroid/enemy spawning **resumes** (R58, R62).
- **AC42 [Play/Smoke]** The boss **enters slowly**, **settles at the vertical centre** of the screen, and
  then **oscillates left/right**; it does not sit off-screen or leave the play area (R59).
- **AC43 [Play/Smoke]** The boss has a **large health pool**; **player shots (Z, incl. Rapid/Fan) damage
  it** via the normal collision path, and after the specified number of hits it is **destroyed** — i.e. the
  boss is always defeatable, never invulnerable to player fire (R60).
- **AC44 [Play]** The boss **damages the player on body/ram contact** and via its **moveset bullets** (the
  yellow fan + red split children), routed through the §V2.7 damage step (honouring i-frames/shield) (R61).
- **AC45 [Play/Smoke]** On **boss defeat**, the player is awarded the **big flat reward** (clearly larger
  than a normal kill) and the **normal loop resumes** until the next breakpoint (R62).
- **AC46 [Play/Smoke]** Pressing **X during a boss fight** spends a charge and **clears the boss's minions +
  all boss/enemy bullets** (per R48 scope); the **boss itself obeys the chosen bomb-vs-boss rule** (immune,
  or the specified chip) — deterministically (R63).
- **AC47 [Play]** While a boss is active, a **boss health bar + label** is shown that **tracks the boss's
  health**, is **visually distinct** from the player HP bar / buff pills / bomb readout, and is **removed on
  defeat** (R64).
- **AC48 [Play/Smoke]** The **Mothership** runs its **4-step moveset in the specified order and cadence**,
  **looping (1→2→3→4→1…) until defeated** (R66).
- **AC49 [Play/Smoke]** The moveset spawns **exactly 5 REGULAR (step 1), 2 HEAVY (step 2), and 7 SCOUT
  (step 3)** — the **v5 enemy kinds** — and those minions **behave as ordinary v5 enemies** (move/fire/take
  damage/score per R36–R40) (R67).
- **AC50 [Play/Smoke]** Attack step 4 fires a **fan of yellow bullets** that **at its midpoint splits into
  exactly 12 red bullets** flying outward **in all directions** (an even ~360° burst); the red children are
  **ordinary red enemy bullets** (flat `EB_DMG`, despawn off-screen) and **never split again** (R68).
- **AC51 [Smoke]** `main.py --smoke-test` **exits 0 after exactly 120 frames** headless (R14/R33/R43/R54
  preserved) **and** the smoke run **exercises a boss encounter** — a **seeded/forced boss** whose
  **arrival field-clear (R57), entrance (R59), and ≥1 moveset attack step (R67/R68)** all run within the
  120 frames **without raising** — with **no AC1–AC38 regression** (R65).
- **AC52 [Code]** The boss **reuses existing machinery**: the **v6 flush/flash** (factored so the arrival
  clear runs **without** the charge-decrement/lockout path), the **v5 enemy roster** for minions, and the
  **v5 green→red split** pattern for attack-4's yellow→red split; the boss, its health bar, and its bullets
  render as **shapes/text only**; the boss health is an explicit integer; **no external assets** are added
  (R57, R60, R64, R67, R68; C2).
