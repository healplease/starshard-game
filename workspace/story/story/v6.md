## V6.2 HUD bomb-count readout — **icon + `×N`** (R45, GDD §V6.2, art_spec §V6.3)

The Artist specced two equally-blessed forms — an **icon `×N`** readout and a spelled **`BOMBS: N`**
label — both locked to the same anchor (top-right, **`right=588, y=34`**, under the HP bar, clear of the
§V2.6 left pill stack). **Writer's pick: the icon + `×N` form** (the Artist's recommendation).

- **On-screen text:** `f"×{charges}"` → e.g. **`×2`** (multiplication sign `×` U+00D7, no space — compact
  in the tight corner). The **violet bomb-sphere icon** to its left *is* the label, so no word "BOMBS" is
  needed on the persistent HUD — this mirrors how the HP **bar** carries the health read without a word.
- **Why `×N` over `BOMBS: N`:** compact (the corner is narrow, under the HP bar), unmistakable once the
  bomb icon labels it, and trivially scales if the cap (4) is ever retuned — exactly the Artist's §V6.3
  rationale. It also reads as a quantity ("times-2 = two bombs"), consistent with the arcade voice.
- **Empty (0) state:** the integer still renders — `×0` — dimmed to `TEXT_DIM` with the icon hollowed to
  a ring (art_spec §V6.3). The text is *correct by construction* (it reads the live `charges`), so it
  needs no special string; `×0` + the dimmed/hollow look is the "out of bombs, X does nothing" read (R47).
- **ASCII degrade:** if a build's font ever lacks `×` (U+00D7), lowercase `x` (`x2`) is an acceptable
  fallback — this is exactly the literal the Artist sketched in the §V6.3 code (`f"x{charges}"`). Glyph
  only; the meaning is identical. The multiplication sign is the intended/canonical form.
- **Blessed fallback wording (if the Programmer prefers spelled-out):** `f"BOMBS: {charges}"` at the same
  anchor — also final and AC-correct. Pick **one**; the icon `×N` is the recommendation.
