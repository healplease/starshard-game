## V12.2 ★ `PAUSE_HINT_RESTART` rewrite — `"Hold R  Restart"` ⚠ REWRITE

> **Old value** (`config.py` line 377): `"R  Restart"`
> **New value**: `"Hold R  Restart"`

**Why:** v8 wrote `"R  Restart"` when R restarted on a single key-press; v12 converts Restart to a
**0.5 s hold gesture** (GDD §V12.5, `RESTART_HOLD_FRAMES = PAUSE_QUIT_FRAMES = 30 f`). Leaving the
old string would imply an instant press — players would tap R, see nothing happen, and read the hold
as broken (the §46 "stale instant-restart copy" risk). The hint must teach the gesture.

- **Mirrors `PAUSE_HINT_QUIT = "Hold Q  Quit"` verbatim in shape** — capital **"Hold"**, key, two
  spaces, action. The two PAUSE hold hints now read as a matched pair (`"Hold Q  Quit"` /
  `"Hold R  Restart"`), and the player who learned the Q-hold instantly reads the R-hold.
- **"Hold R" describes the gesture, not just the key** — signals a tap won't fire; deliberate
  resistance (0.5 s) is required, mirroring the sweeping arc the Artist anchors below it.
- **Stays ONE FONT_SMALL line at its locked top-y 418** (art_spec §V12.5.2 / §V12.6) — the string
  only grows **horizontally**; it never wraps or moves in y, so the R arc at `(200, 483)` keeps its
  proven 25 px vertical clearance regardless of width.
- **Width:** measured **108 px** at `FONT_SMALL` 22 against the `W = 600` budget — well inside (was
  the 56 px `"R  Restart"`). ✓ Centred at x=300 (the bottom-most PAUSE hint, art_spec §V12.6).
- **No stale instant-restart copy remains** — the literal now reads "Hold R" (AC75). ✓
