## V12.1 The decision in one line

The reused v8 progress arc is drawn for the R-restart gesture at **`(200, 483)` on PAUSE** and
**`(200, 545)` on GAME_OVER** — each the screen's locked **Q-arc centre shifted 100 px LEFT, at the
same y** (a 44×44 box). This puts the R arc in the **same open lower band** as the Q arc, **56 px**
(rect-edge to rect-edge) to its left — clearly two distinct gauges — and proven clear of every text
rect on its screen. Idle-visibility **matches the Q arc per screen**: on **PAUSE** the R track is
**always on** (so PAUSE hosts two always-on empty tracks, side by side); on **GAME_OVER** the whole R
widget is drawn **only while `r_hold_frames > 0`**.

## V12.9 Draw recipe — reuse the generalised arc helper, two guarded R calls

The Programmer generalises the v8/v10 arc body into **`draw_hold_arc(screen, center, hold_frames,
threshold)`** (GDD §V12.11 / §V12.7.1) and calls it for **both** the Q and R arcs with the matching
counter + threshold. The R-arc calls (paste-ready, mirroring v10's `draw_*_quit_arc`):

```python
def draw_pause_restart_arc(screen, r_hold_frames):
    """PAUSE: R-restart arc — track ALWAYS on (matches the Q arc on PAUSE), fill while
    r_hold_frames > 0. (art_spec v12 §V12.3) Drawn inside / alongside draw_pause."""
    draw_hold_arc(screen, C.PAUSE_RESTART_ARC_CENTER,
                  r_hold_frames, C.RESTART_HOLD_FRAMES)   # track always drawn by the helper


def draw_gameover_restart_arc(screen, r_hold_frames):
    """GAME_OVER: draw the whole R widget ONLY while R is held (art_spec v12 §V12.3),
    mirroring draw_gameover_quit_arc."""
    if r_hold_frames > 0:
        draw_hold_arc(screen, C.GAMEOVER_RESTART_ARC_CENTER,
                      r_hold_frames, C.RESTART_HOLD_FRAMES)
```

- **PAUSE** (track always on): fold the R arc into `draw_pause` (or call `draw_pause_restart_arc`
  unconditionally after it) so its `HP_BACK` track draws every PAUSE frame at `(200,483)`, beside the
  existing Q track at `(300,483)`. The Q arc's PAUSE behaviour is **unchanged**.
- **GAME_OVER** (only while held): after `hud.draw_gameover(...)` and the existing
  `hud.draw_gameover_quit_arc(...)`, call `draw_gameover_restart_arc(screen, self.r_hold_frames)` at
  `(200,545)`.
- `draw_hold_arc` reuses `PAUSE_ARC_R`, `PAUSE_ARC_STROKE`, `HP_AMBER`, `HP_BACK` verbatim — **no new
  arc constants** (GDD §V12.11).
