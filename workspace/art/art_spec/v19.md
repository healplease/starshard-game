## V19a.2 The SHIFT red hitbox indicator (R117)
A red circle marking the player's **true damage hitbox**, drawn on the ship **only while a SHIFT key is held
in PLAY** (per §V19.5: it disappears the frame SHIFT releases; never outside PLAY). Render-only — changes no
collision (GDD §V19.5 / AC106).

- **Radius = the real hitbox.** Draw at `P_HITBOX_R` — *the exact collision constant the Level-designer
  locks* (≈50% of `P_R`=13, GDD §V19.4). The indicator and the hitbox are **one number**: if LD retunes the
  radius, this follows automatically. Do **not** introduce a separate "indicator radius."
- **Shape:** a **filled disc** at `P_HITBOX_R`, fill `HITBOX_RED` @ `HITBOX_ALPHA` (128), **plus a 1-px fully
  opaque (`alpha=255`) `HITBOX_RED` rim** on the circle's edge. Rationale: at the small hitbox radius (~6–7 px)
  a 50% fill over the **cyan** ship desaturates toward mauve; the opaque rim keeps the *exact collision
  boundary* crisp and unmistakably red. The rim sits **on** the radius (it marks the true edge, not outside it).
- **Blend:** **straight alpha**, NOT additive. Draw onto a small per-pixel `pygame.Surface((d,d),
  pygame.SRCALPHA)` (d = `2*P_HITBOX_R + 2`) with the alpha baked into the draw color `(255,40,64,128)` / rim
  `(255,40,64,255)`, then `screen.blit` centered on `(player.x, player.y)`. (Additive over cyan would wash to
  white/pink and lose the "red" read; the v11 `SRCALPHA`/`set_alpha` gotcha applies — bake alpha into the
  color, don't `set_alpha` a non-SRCALPHA surface.)
- **Position:** centered on the ship origin `(player.x, player.y)` — the same center the collision uses.
- **When:** `state == PLAY` **and** (`K_LSHIFT` or `K_RSHIFT` held). Nothing drawn otherwise.

## V19a.3 ~50%-larger bullets — the draw==collision invariant (R116)
**Invariant (the v19 fairness keystone, GDD §V19.6): per family, the drawn body radius == the family's
collision radius.** This **retires the old render-only draw≠collision flourishes** (pellet drew 8 vs collide
5; YELLOW hardcoded 6 vs 5; NOVA 6 vs 5) — going forward what you see is exactly what hits you, mirroring the
player's now-honest small core. **Collision magnitudes are the Level-designer's to lock; the drawn sizes below
are bound to equal them** — recommended ~1.5× targets:

| Family | Today (draw / collision) | v19 draw = collision (recommended ~1.5×) | Drawn as |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Player bullet** | rect `4×12` / circle `r=PB_H/2=6` | `PB_W,PB_H = 6,18` → collision `r=PB_H/2=9` | rect `PB_W×PB_H`, `BULLET_P` (unchanged shape) |
| **Enemy aimed (RED)** | `r=5` / `5` | `EB_R = 8` | filled circle `r=EB_R`, `EB_COLOR_RED` |
| **RED split children** | `r=5` / `5` | `EB_R = 8` (same family) | filled circle `r=EB_R`, `EB_COLOR_RED` |
| **HEAVY pellet (GREEN id)** | `r=8` / `5` | `r=EB_R = 8` | filled circle `r=EB_R`, `EB_COLOR_PURPLE` (v17 hue) |
| **SCOUT streak (CYAN)** | head `4` + tail `12` / `5` | head `r=EB_R=8`; tail `CYAN_TAIL_LEN = 18` | head circle `r=EB_R` + render-only motion tail |
| **Boss fan (YELLOW)** | `r=6` (hardcoded) / `5` | `r=EB_R = 8` | filled circle `r=EB_R`, `EB_COLOR_YELLOW` |
| **NOVA plasma** | `r=6` / `5` | `r=EB_R = 8` | filled circle `r=EB_R`, `NOVA_BULLET` |

Notes:
- **All circular enemy/boss families now draw at the single shared collision radius `EB_R`** — so the
  programmer draws each at `EB_R` and retires `PELLET_DRAW_R` / `NOVA_BULLET_DRAW_R` / the hardcoded `6` (and
  RED's literal `EB_R` draw stays correct by construction). `EB_R`: **5 → 8** (≈1.5×; 7.5 rounded up to 8).
- **Pellet "fat telegraph":** with draw==collision it no longer reads *bigger* than other bullets — its
  identity is carried by its **purple `#D230DC` (v17)** hue, not an inflated radius. *If* LD prefers to keep
  the pellet visibly fattest, LD may lock a **larger per-family collision** for it; the pellet's draw then
  equals that larger radius (invariant holds). Default = the shared `EB_R` (simplest, lowest-risk).
- **CYAN tail** stays a **render-only motion streak** (clearly a trail, not the body); its head dot = the
  collision circle `r=EB_R`. Scale the tail ~1.5× (`CYAN_TAIL_LEN 12→18`) so proportions track the bigger head.
- **Player bullet:** the collision circle is `r=PB_H/2` (height-derived), so scaling the rect scales the
  hitbox in lockstep; width `PB_W` is draw-only. No shape/color change.
- Watch-items (flagged for LD/QA, not my call): no tunneling, no false off-screen culling — bullet **speeds**
  unchanged (R110/v18 untouched).

## V19a.4 Render order — one new top world-layer (appends §V2.6)
The hitbox indicator marks the player and must stay crisp for precision play, so it draws **after particles,
just below the HUD** — nothing in the play field occludes it. Bullet size changes need **no** order change
(same layers, same call sites, larger radii).

```
starfield → asteroids + enemy bullets → bonus diamonds → player bullets → enemies
  → player (+ shield ring) → particles → ★HITBOX INDICATOR (PLAY + SHIFT-held only)★ → HUD
```
This appends one conditional layer to the v2 §V2.6 order; all prior layers are unchanged.
